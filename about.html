Hi, I'm Camille.

I grew up in small towns in Vermont and New Hampshire but now live in Chicago, studying to get my Bachelor’s degree in Interactive and Social Media and my Master’s degree in Human Computer Interaction at DePaul University.


I stumbled into the field of user experience design in college and realized I want to create things that help people. Not only do I love creating designs, but I really enjoy writing the code as well. Whether it be creating sketches and prototypes or writing HTML, CSS, and Javascript, I find joy in knowing that my efforts will benefit someone.


I believe that things should be created for a purpose, and when I get to be a part of realizing that purpose, I know that the final product will be meaningful not only to the clients, but to the users as well.
